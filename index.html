About Us

OrangeGrey Creative & Communications Agency is a dynamic Strategic Marketing Communications, Public Relations, Media Buying and Events organisation, with a recognized niche in Social and Behavioural Change Communication (SBCC). We empower social change by helping organizations and individuals that work towards dignified social experiences of individuals and communities, to achieve the change they seek among beneficiary targets through strategic Social and Behavioural Change Communications (SBCC). We achieve this by going beyond demographics to leveraging in-depth audience insights to uncover motivations, contextual biases, attitudes, and beliefs that fuel social issues and proffer solutions that drive change through inclusive and affirmative action for real social impact.

Vision

To make the world a better place for dignified human experiences and the catalyst for a world where empowered individuals and communities drive positive social change through informed action and shared ownership of progressive actions.

Our Approach

We provide SBCC Technical Advisory and Consultancy services that is audience-centric through primary and extended stakeholders’ consultations, data-driven insights, collaborative partnerships, trainings, and customized targeted media campaigns that resonates with the beneficiary communities and make the world a better place.

Our Mission

To achieve social change through strategic integrated behavioural change communication by crafting impactful messages that is dedicated to: Empowering individuals: We equip people with the knowledge, resources, and confidence to become active participants in social improvement. Igniting ownership: We enhance progressive ownership of social change by fostering understanding, amplifying local voices, and promoting collaborative solutions through stakeholder consultations, social enterprises and equipping for sustainable change.
Inspiring action: Through integrated strategic communication tactics, we create compelling messages that motivate individuals and communities to take action and contribute to a better future for people and the environment
